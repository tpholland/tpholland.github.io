Myshowcase.me for Careers Services

Support your students with a winning set of tools



Myshowcase.me is a stunningly simple tool that helps your students prepare for the future and showcase employability from day one.


It's the best answer to the question of how your grads can demonstrate everything they've learned, showcase the capabilities their course has given them, and demonstrate what they can offer potential employers. All of their experiences, structured in a way that lets them shine - and infinitely flexible, to fit the needs of any employer or recruiter.


  • Prepare students for future workplace challenges and help them demonstrate employability from day one.

  • Real value for your grads: a stunningly simple way to store and showcase achievements past graduation.

  • Engage students with something stunning: a personal offering to showcase their individual strengths.

  • A practical solution that helps students find that dream job.


Transform your offering with a tool that focusses your students on employability throughout their studies and beyond.
