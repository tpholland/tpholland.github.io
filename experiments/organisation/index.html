Myshowcase.me for Organisations



Myshowcase.me for Organisations is a new kind of eportfolio. Showcase users’ achievements and experiences. Capture experiences from the cloud to produce a multi-dimensional view.


Organisations use Myshowcase.me to guide and monitor users as they gather and build evidence to meet objectives and targets, allowing for the presentation of personal capabilities and projects by managing the sharing of documents, photos, videos and more in a single showcase. Reporting tools provide a real time picture of progress.

Keep up to date with the latest Myshowcase.me news.


  • Allow digital information to flow more easily through your organisation.

  • Users can prepare personal capability statements supported by evidence from a wide range of sources.

  • Users can also prepare a summary of their personal project activity.

  • Encourage users to continue to build and grow a complete picture of their working life.

  • Provide a structure for the presentation of achievements gained and goals met.

  • Provide a radical alternative or enhancement to traditional academic eportfolios.


It’s never too soon to start collecting achievements to support future career growth and development.






Myshowcase.me Supports Skills and Development



Supporting Individuals and taking steps to assist them throughout their career journey is a commitment that your Organisations can make with Myshowcase.me.



The user’s journey may start at University, in employment, a professional place of study or anywhere there is a requirement and a need to provide the resources to achieve future potential. Individuals are also able to be guided in best practice with Myshowcase.me

Through the inclusion of any digitally stored achievement users are able to present professional showcases that focus on their individual capabilities at every stage of their career. Tutors and managers are able to assist through the use of competency and curriculum frameworks and reporting tools to build and develop relevant achievements.




Myshowcase.me at Work



Employer engagement is important to both individuals preparing for their initial foray into the workplace, as well as promoting achievements gained once at work, Myshowcase.me supports the user throughout.



Competition for positions in an organisation whether for new applicants or existing employees is always strong – as an employer it’s necessary to effectively determine an individual’s suitability for a role based on their capabilities and achievements. Myshowcase.me includes a customisable Capability Statement template that can help ensure professional showcases are produced and presented every time.

Engaging with other parties including clients/customers/third parties often requires an organisation to promote its self – again Myshowcase.me allows for relevant achievements and activities of Individuals, Groups/Teams to be professionally showcased.





The system is flexible enough to meet the requirements of different types of individuals and user organisations.
